Privacy Policy — Fillup: Shared Shopping Lists

Last updated: March 2026

Fillup ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Fillup mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name: A display name you provide when first using the app.
  • Phone number: Used to identify you when other users invite you to shared lists.
  • Push notification token: Used to send you list invitation notifications.
  • Shopping list data: Items, categories, and statuses you create within the app.
  • Usage data: Anonymous, aggregated statistics about how the app is used (e.g. feature usage, session frequency). This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable real-time shared shopping lists between users.
  • To send push notifications when you are invited to a list.
  • To identify you when another user invites you by phone number.
  • To analyze anonymous usage statistics in order to improve the app and develop new features.
  • Aggregated, anonymized statistical data may be shared with or sold to third parties. This data contains no personally identifiable information.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database operated by Google LLC, on servers located in Europe (europe-west1).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Aggregated and anonymized statistical data (which cannot identify you) may be shared with or sold to third parties for research or business purposes.

Your name is visible to other members of shared lists you join or create. Your phone number is used only for matching you with list invitations and is not publicly displayed.

5. Contacts Permission

The app may request access to your contacts to help you invite people to shared lists. Contact data is used only locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Fillup is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
